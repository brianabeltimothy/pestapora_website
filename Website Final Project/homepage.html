ABOUT THE FESTIVAL


Pestapora is the latest celebration of Indonesian music performances, organized by Boss Creator. This first music celebration will be present for three days, on 23, 24 and 25 September 2022 which will be located at Gambir Expo Kemayoran, Jakarta.


There are 6 stage performances with a variety of menus. Not to forget, the area of pleasure-enhancing supplements which consists of; Food & Beverages Area, Market Place, Happy Place, and several other entertainment areas.


Three days full of joy, let's have a Pestapora!
